# Privacy Policy — Ngolf - ParTracker

Last updated: July 2025

## Data Collection
Ngolf - ParTracker does not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers.

## Data Storage
All golf round data, scores, and player information are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

## iCloud
If you use iCloud on your device, your data may be backed up through Apple's iCloud service. This is controlled by your device's iCloud settings.

## Analytics
We do not use any third-party analytics tools.

## Subscriptions
Subscription purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.
